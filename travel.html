Travel Do's and Don'ts

Follow these essential travel tips for a safe, enjoyable, and responsible journey across Ratnagiri district.

Travel Do's

  • Respect local culture and traditions.
  • Keep beaches and tourist spots clean.
  • Carry reusable water bottles to reduce plastic waste.
  • Try authentic Konkani food from trusted places.
  • Book hotels in advance during peak seasons.
  • Wear comfortable clothing suited to coastal weather.
  • Support local businesses and artisans.
  • Follow safety instructions at beaches and forts.

Travel Don'ts

  • Do not litter or damage natural surroundings.
  • Avoid entering restricted or unsafe areas.
  • Do not disturb wildlife or marine life.
  • Avoid loud music in peaceful locations.
  • Do not ignore weather warnings during monsoon.
  • Avoid risky water activities without proper guidance.
  • Do not disrespect religious places.
  • Avoid overpacking — travel light.
